ABOUT US

At TUFIKIANE, we are dedicated to improving the lives of children in need by connecting compassionate donors with opportunities to make a lasting impact.
We aim to provide essential support and resources to children's homes, ensuring that every child has the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential.

OUR STORY

Founded in 2024, TUFIKIANE emerged from a shared passion for making a difference in the lives of vulnerable children.
Our journey began with a small group of volunteers who witnessed the challenges faced by children's homes in providing adequate care and support.
Determined to make a positive change, we set out to create a platform that would connect caring individuals with opportunities to support these children in meaningful ways.

IMPACT

Through the generosity of our donors and the dedication of our partners, TUFIKIANE has been able to make a tangible difference in the lives of countless children.
From improving living conditions and educational opportunities to supporting emotional well-being and personal development, our initiatives have empowered children to overcome adversity and pursue their dreams.

TRANSPARENCY

At TUFIKIANE, we believe in transparency and accountability in everything we do. We are committed to ensuring that donations are used effectively and
efficiently to benefit the children we serve. As part of our commitment to transparency, we provide regular updates, financial reports, and impact assessments
to our donors and stakeholders.
